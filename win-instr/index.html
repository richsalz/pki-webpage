Windows Instructions

Once you install the IBM SSL CA, your browser will automatically trust every certificate that this CA generates. Do you trust the IBMers who run it? Do you trust that nobody stole the keys or otherwise let an outsider in? If the answer is yes, then here is what you need to do.

First, click on the "Get Certificate" link in the other window. You will see a prompt like this. Click on "Open."


This will bring up prompt that lets you examine the certificate and install it if you want:


Click on "Install Certificate..." to bring up the import wizard:


Click on "Next" to specify where to store the certificate:


Let windows automatically do the right thing and click on "Next":


Verify that you told Windows to install a certificate in the right place and click "Finish." You will now get one last security warning. If you are moderately paranoid, you can verify that the thumbprint in your popup matches the one below:
